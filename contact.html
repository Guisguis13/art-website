To Contact Vance:

Contact Me For:

Please reach out to me by contacting via phone or email. I would love hear what you have to think about my artwork. I'd also love to hear your own unique story and maybe even hear about your journey as a fellow artist. Of course you can contact me for Commission Requests as well, this can include a piece that you have always dreamed of, tattoo worthy artwork, unqiue wedding gifts, murals, coffee mugs, T-Shirts, and much more. Ask! I will do my best to fulfill your vision!

Commission Request Information:

For a 'Commission Request' please ensure you give adequate information. For example: Basic Personal Information, Subject Matter, Size, Themes, Budget and Timeframe (*Note* Deadlines are estimates. Artwork will be completed in a timely manner, without jeopardizing the quailty of work). Thank you in advance for your inquiries.
